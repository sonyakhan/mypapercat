Marvel Entertainment

For my internship at Marvel Entertainment in the digital comics department, I co-produced two printed comic books: “A Year of Marvels: The Uncanny #1” and “Deadpool: Too Soon?”, working exclusively on the lettering and paneling. I also designed several variant book covers for “The Invincible Iron Man: Disassembled Prologue” and digitally composed printed comics into web form, working exclusively on the UX of Marvel Unlimited.
