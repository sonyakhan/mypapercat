Admerasia

At Admerasia, I worked on the storyboard and logo design for client Nissan. Two of my storyboards were approved for production and aired on Nissan's YouTube channel. The campaign was targeted at Chinese-Americans, using YouTube celebrities and local fashion icons.
In addition, I designed an infographic piece for Asia Society visualizing a coporate survey for Asian Pacific Americans and the logo for the New York Online Newsletter.
